Al Falah Food Bank



What is Al Falah Food Bank?

Al Falah Food Bank has been around since 2019, with the sole aim of combatting poverty within London. The need for foodbanks in London is on the rise, with many struggling to meet basic living costs. Access to clean, healthy food is a basic human right, which unfortunately many are deprived of. The Al Falah Food Bank works to provide those most in need with essentials we all need.
